Binance & Bybit P2P Bot

Why do you need an automated P2P trading bot?

In the P2P trading market, manual operations are making you lose competitiveness:

  • High price volatility: Manual price adjustments can never keep up with the 24/7 rapidly changing market.
  • Low exposure: Once your ad drops out of the Top 3, your transaction volume can fall off a cliff.

This open-source / tool project is designed to solve these pain points and help you achieve 24/7 automated bidding.
